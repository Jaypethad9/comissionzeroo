Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a tender as a customer?

Navigate to your dashboard and click "Create New Tender." Fill in details about your project including service type, location, deadline, and description. Professionals will then be able to view and submit quotes for your consideration.

What verification process do professionals undergo?

All service providers must submit proof of licensure (where applicable), insurance documentation, and portfolio examples. Our team manually reviews each application to ensure quality standards are met before approval.

How does the commission-free model work?

Unlike other platforms, we never take a percentage from professionals' earnings. They pay a small flat monthly fee for access to the platform, allowing them to keep 100% of what they earn from jobs.

What payment methods are accepted?

Customers pay professionals directly through their preferred method (cash, check, or digital payment). CommissionZero doesn't handle payments, which helps us maintain our zero-commission model.

How do I report an issue with a service professional?

Please contact our support team immediately with details of the issue. We take all reports seriously and will investigate while working to mediate a fair resolution for all parties involved.
